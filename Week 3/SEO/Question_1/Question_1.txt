1. What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
•	SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization.
•	It helps you optimize our websites so that they have better rankings on Search engines like Google, Bing and Yahoo.
•	The ultimate goal of SEO is to rank higher in search engine
•	results pages for relevant keywords and phrases, which can increase visibility, traffic, and ultimately revenue for the websites.
•	It is essential for businesses that want to succeed in the digital age.
•	Meta tags are important for SEO because they provide information to search engines about the content on your site and can help
•	improve your website's visibility and ranking in search engine results pages

Importance of SEO:
•	Increased Visibility: SEO helps your website appear higher in search engine results for relevant keywords and phrases.
 When your site ranks higher, it's more likely to be seen by users, leading to increased organic traffic.
•	Credibility and Trust: Websites that rank well in search results are often perceived as more credible and trustworthy 
by users. People tend to trust search engines, so appearing near the top of results can boost your website's credibility.
•	Improved User Experience: SEO involves optimizing website structure, content, and navigation, which can enhance the 
overall user experience. When users find your site easy to navigate and the content valuable, they are more likely to stay longer and return in the future.
•	Targeted Traffic: SEO allows you to target specific keywords and phrases that are relevant to your business or content. 
This helps attract users who are actively searching for information, products, or services related to what you offer, increasing the likelihood of conversion.
•	Cost-Effective Marketing: Compared to paid advertising, SEO can be a cost-effective long-term strategy for driving 
traffic to your website. Once you achieve good rankings, you don't have to pay for clicks like you would in a pay-per-click (PPC) campaign.
•	Competitive Advantage: Your competitors are likely investing in SEO. By optimizing 
your website, you can stay competitive or even outperform them in search results, gaining a competitive edge in your industry.
•	Local Visibility: For businesses with physical locations, local SEO is crucial. It helps 
businesses appear in local search results, which is especially important for attracting nearby customers.
•	Measurable Results: SEO efforts can be tracked and analyzed using various tools and 
analytics platforms. This allows you to measure the effectiveness of your strategies and make data-driven decisions for continuous improvement.
•	Adaptation to Algorithm Changes: Search engines continually update their algorithms. SEO professionals stay up to date with these changes and adjust strategies accordingly to maintain or improve rankings.
•	Global Reach: SEO can help businesses and websites reach a global audience. With proper
 optimization, your content can be discovered by users around the world.
