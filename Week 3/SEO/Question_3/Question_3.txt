What are the benefits of using meta tag?
1. SEO Boost: Meta tags improve search engine ranking.
2. User Engagement: They provide clear, concise information for users.
3. Credibility: Well-crafted tags build trust with users.
4. Social Sharing: Enable better sharing on social media.
5. Mobile Optimization: Enhance the mobile user experience.
6. Accessibility: Ensure content is accessible to all.
7. Analytics: Gather valuable user data.
8. Localization: Tailor content to specific audiences.
9. Security: Protect your site and users.
